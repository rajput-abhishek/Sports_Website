SPORT ACADEMY is a not-for-profit organisation working to preserve, share, educate and celebrate youth culture history through a passionate network of photographers and creatives. At SPORT ACADEMY, we believe in the power of youth culture heritage as a catalyst for creativity, self expression and connecting communities. We have observed a continual and growing demand for youth culture to be preserved, shared and ultimately celebrated - connecting the past, present and future generations. We celebrate youth culture through curated events, exhibitions, collaborations and consultancy. Our images, features and essays are a real reflection of Nepalese and International people's lives, celebrating self expression, energy and passion. Content featured includes: fashion, music, art, travel, genres, social issues, lifestyles, sports, nightlife, environments, humour and much much more.!
